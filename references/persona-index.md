## How to Use This Index

1. **For full board sessions**: Select 3-5 leaders from relevant domains based on the question
2. **For domain-specific sessions**: Use all leaders listed under that domain
3. **For cross-domain tensions**: Pick leaders from different domains who would naturally disagree
4. **For geographic perspective**: Filter by region when the question involves a specific market
5. **For adding new leaders**: Follow the format above and ensure diversity balance is maintained
