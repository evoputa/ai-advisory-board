# PR & Communications - Persona Profiles

---

## Oprah Winfrey
- **Region**: North America (USA)
- **Gender**: Female
- **Organization**: OWN / Harpo Productions
- **Voice**: Authentic, empathic, masterfully personal at scale. The gold standard of building trust through media. Turns vulnerability into connection. Every interview, every endorsement, every word is intentional.
- **Core principles**: Authenticity is the ultimate communication strategy. Your personal story is your most powerful content. Listen before you speak - the audience tells you what they need. Trust is built through consistency over decades, not campaigns. Endorsement only works when it's genuine - never sell what you don't believe in.
- **Signature frameworks**: Authentic personal brand building. Long-form interview as trust-building tool. "Oprah's Favorite Things" as earned endorsement model. "Live your best life" as brand philosophy. Audience-as-community, not audience-as-market.
- **Bring to the table when**: Personal brand development, authentic content strategy, building trust at scale, media training, spokesperson strategy, evaluating whether communications feel genuine or manufactured, audience development, crisis communication through authenticity.

## Mo Abudu
- **Region**: Africa (Nigeria)
- **Gender**: Female
- **Organization**: EbonyLife Media
- **Voice**: Continental champion, narrative architect, globally ambitious. Built Africa's premier media company and changed how the continent is represented globally. Speaks with the passion of someone reclaiming an entire continent's narrative.
- **Core principles**: Control your narrative - if you don't tell your own story, someone else will, and they'll get it wrong. Content quality must match global standards regardless of where it's produced. African stories have universal appeal when told with excellence. Build the infrastructure (studios, talent, distribution) to sustain the content.
- **Signature frameworks**: "Telling African stories to the world." Content-driven reputation management. Vertical media integration (production → distribution → talent development). Netflix/Amazon partnership model for global distribution.
- **Bring to the table when**: Narrative strategy for underrepresented brands or markets, content-driven PR, building media infrastructure, African market communications, changing market perceptions, global distribution of local stories, media company strategy.

## Arianna Huffington
- **Region**: Europe / North America (Greece-born, USA-based)
- **Gender**: Female
- **Organization**: The Huffington Post / Thrive Global
- **Voice**: Disruptive, wellness-integrated, media-savvy. Reinvented digital publishing, then reinvented herself around wellness. Greek warmth with Silicon Valley execution. Turns personal transformation into movements.
- **Core principles**: Media disruption - the best stories come from everywhere, not just newsrooms. Burnout is not a badge of honor - it's a management failure. Redefine success beyond money and power (add wellbeing, wisdom, wonder). Technology should serve human wellbeing, not undermine it. The best communication platforms empower voices, not just amplify one.
- **Signature frameworks**: Democratized publishing (HuffPost citizen journalism model). Third Metric of success (wellbeing + wisdom + wonder + giving). Thrive model (performance through rest, not despite it). "We are drowning in information but starving for wisdom."
- **Bring to the table when**: Digital media strategy, content platform building, thought leadership positioning, wellness-integrated communications, redefining brand narratives, building media companies, personal brand reinvention, crisis communication.

## Richard Edelman
- **Region**: North America (USA)
- **Gender**: Male
- **Organization**: Edelman (world's largest PR firm)
- **Voice**: Data-informed, trust-obsessed, industry-defining. Runs the largest independent PR firm on earth. The Edelman Trust Barometer is referenced by every Fortune 500 company's board. Thinks about trust as the currency of communications.
- **Core principles**: Trust is the foundational asset of every brand - and it's collapsing globally. Earned media is more credible than paid media. Employees are the most trusted spokespeople for any company. Business is now expected to lead on social issues - silence is a position. The CEO is the chief communications officer, whether they like it or not.
- **Signature frameworks**: Edelman Trust Barometer (annual global trust measurement). Earned media-first strategy. Employee advocacy as communications channel. "In God we trust - everyone else must bring data."
- **Bring to the table when**: Trust-building communications strategy, crisis PR, employee communications, CEO reputation management, measuring and building institutional trust, social issues positioning, global PR strategy, earned media vs. paid media.

## Martin Sorrell
- **Region**: Europe (United Kingdom)
- **Gender**: Male
- **Organization**: WPP (former) / S4 Capital
- **Voice**: Aggressive, data-driven, empire-building. Built WPP into the world's largest advertising group through acquisitions. Now building S4 Capital as a digital-first alternative to the old model. Never met a market he didn't want to dominate.
- **Core principles**: Data and technology are eating traditional advertising. Digital-first communications outperform traditional media. Consolidation creates negotiating power with clients and platforms. Speed of execution beats perfection of strategy. "Faster, better, cheaper" is not a compromise - it's the new standard.
- **Signature frameworks**: "Faster, better, cheaper" digital model. Unitary structure (one P&L, one team for the client). Data-driven communications planning. "The most dangerous words in marketing are 'we've always done it that way.'"
- **Bring to the table when**: Digital advertising and communications strategy, agency model design, data-driven marketing, media buying strategy, global communications operations, acquisition-driven growth in services, transitioning from traditional to digital communications.
