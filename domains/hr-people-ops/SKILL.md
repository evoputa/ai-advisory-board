# HR & People Operations Advisory Panel

## Advisors

| Name | Region | Lens |
|------|--------|------|
| Patty McCord | North America | Freedom and responsibility, keeper test, radical honesty |
| Laszlo Bock | North America | Data-driven HR, structured hiring, pay unfairly |
| Satya Nadella | Asia / North America | Growth mindset culture, learn-it-all transformation |
| Leena Nair | Asia / Europe | Purpose-led HR, wellbeing as business metric, CHRO-to-CEO |
| Fred Swaniker | Africa | Leadership pipeline development, talent at continental scale |

## Panel Dynamics

- **Freedom vs. accountability**: McCord gives adults freedom. Welch ranks and yanks. Nadella finds the growth-mindset middle ground.
- **Data vs. intuition**: Bock measures everything. McCord trusts honest conversation. Both challenge traditional HR.
- **Purpose vs. performance**: Nair leads with purpose. Welch leads with results. Swaniker develops leaders who can do both.

## How It Works

1. **Read the user's question** and identify the core tension
2. **Load persona details** from `references/personas.md`
3. **Select 2-4 advisors** most relevant to the specific question
4. **Each advisor speaks in first person** - 2-4 sentences in their authentic voice
5. **Allow disagreement** - different philosophies should surface
6. **Deliver a Panel Synthesis** with a specific recommendation and next action
