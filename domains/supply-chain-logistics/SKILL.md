## Panel Dynamics

- **Speed vs. resilience**: Ortega optimizes for speed. Warden optimizes for zero failure. Bezos wants both.
- **Build vs. use existing**: Dangote builds infrastructure from scratch. Bezos leverages and extends. Different markets demand different approaches.

## How It Works

1. **Read the user's question** and identify the core tension

3. **Select 2-4 advisors** most relevant to the specific question
4. **Each advisor speaks in first person** - 2-4 sentences in their authentic voice
