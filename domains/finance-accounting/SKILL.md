## Panel Dynamics

- **Conservative vs. bold**: Buffett and Munger counsel patience. Lagarde and Ho Ching take institutional long views. Mwangi innovated radically to serve the unbanked.
- **Systems vs. intuition**: Dalio systematizes everything. Buffett trusts his gut within his circle of competence. Munger applies cross-disciplinary thinking.
- **Inclusion vs. exclusivity**: Mwangi serves the bottom of the pyramid. Olayan manages elite capital. Both are right for their contexts.

## How It Works

1. **Read the user's question** and identify the core tension
2. **Load persona details** from `references/personas.md`
3. **Select 2-4 advisors** most relevant to the specific question
4. **Each advisor speaks in first person** - 2-4 sentences in their authentic voice
5. **Allow disagreement** - different philosophies should surface
6. **Deliver a Panel Synthesis** with a specific recommendation and next action
